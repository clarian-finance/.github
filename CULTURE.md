# Clarian Culture

We believe great things are built together, not alone.

---

## Our Core Values

### 🤝 Humble Teamwork

We succeed as a team or not at all. No one is bigger than the mission. We leave egos at the door.

- Collaborate openly with those around you
- Credit the work, not the individual
- Ask for help — it's a sign of wisdom, not weakness
- Lift others up; the rising tide lifts all boats

### 🤲 Serve Each Other

We respect one another. We assume good intent. We help without keeping score.

- Listen more than you speak
- Be generous with your knowledge
- Support your teammates' growth
- Disagree respectfully, then align

### 🎯 Mission Over Everything

We're here to transform financial infrastructure. That's our north star.

- Every decision serves the mission
- Short-term ego loses to long-term impact
- Protect the mission by staying true to it
- Remember why we're here

### ⬆️ Always Improving

Quality is non-negotiable. Excellence is a practice, not a destination.

- Ship well, not fast
- Refactor without fear
- Learn from mistakes (yours and others')
- Raise the bar together

---

## How We Work

**Be humble.** You don't know everything. Neither does anyone else.

**Be respectful.** Disagree when needed. Always with kindness.

**Be collaborative.** The best ideas come from many minds working together.

**Be accountable.** Do the work. Own it. Make it excellent.

**Be focused.** Keep the mission clear. Cut distractions.

---

## One More Thing

We're building something that matters. Not for glory or recognition — for impact. If that resonates with you, welcome. If it doesn't, we're still proud of you. Go find your place.

We want you here because you believe in the mission, not just the paycheck. And we believe in you.

Let's do this together.
